ABOUT US

FLORES SANTILLANA S.A.S

MISSION

Flores Santillana S.A.S, is a company dedicated to the production and export of flowers and foliage. It seeks to meet the needs and expectations of its customers by providing high quality products. We achieve this through a principled organization; with motivated, trained staff, with a sense of belonging, teamwork and commitment, achieving profitability and sustainability over time.

VISION

To be leaders in the floriculture market, achieve growth in infrastructure, personnel and production. Systematize the processes to obtain better results. Stand out for the excellent service, commitment and teamwork.

OBJECTIVES

  • To be leaders in the production and marketing of flowers and foliage.
  • Generate and maintain throughout the organization a culture of customer service with high quality standards.
  • Obtain profitability that allows the company to compete efficiently.
  • Improve Productivity.
  • Provide an organizational climate that facilitates the permanent development of its human resources.
